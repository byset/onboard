Taking Office: Final Steps

Congratulations on your election to the Presidency. We’re looking forward to supporting your administration. Before you take office, please tend to a few small tasks: provide an emergency contact, complete a packing list, and sign the oath of office.

This should take only 5–10 minutes. You’ll need: 

  • Basic information about the person you choose to be your emergency contact
  • Access to your clothing items and luggage
